Milestone 1 for Jonathan Harada

Purpose

The purpose of my personal website project is to create a customizable virtual resume/portfolio which will demonstrate my knowledge of front end web development to potential employers. This project should demonstrate high technical ability utilizing Javascript, display a highly empathetic approach to the UI/UX, and be neat and easy to read for any potential employers. I also intend to make this project easily customizable, so that future completed projects can be displayed as my coding career continues.

Audience

The target audience is first and foremost meant to be a professional display for potential employers. This may include: Start-up tech companies either locally or nation wide; local restaurants and other businesses looking to increase their presence online through advertisement/ordering; my networks of friends and family who will view it and may pass it along to a contact; and recruiters.

Goal

The primary goal of this personal website project is to obtain full time employment in the web development field. Secondary goal would be to utilize the project as advertisement of my work in order to obtain short term freelance work.
